Celebrating Excellence in NC Public Schools

Welcome to the Achieving Educational Excellence dashboard, your window into how North Carolina’s public schools are progressing toward being the best in the United States by 2030. This is where we share our work openly with the people of North Carolina and every partner who has joined us on this journey. Below, you will find our Best in Nation Measures, the statewide indicators we are tracking to determine whether we are delivering on the promise of public education for every child. Use the drop-down menu to explore each measure individually, review current data and see progress over time. This dashboard is a living tool, updated regularly as new data becomes available, because transparency is essential to shared ownership of this plan.

Why It Counts

Graduation rate is the most widely recognized measure of a public school system's success. Every student who earns a diploma gains access to more career opportunities, higher lifetime earnings, and better health outcomes.
